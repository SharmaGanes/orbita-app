## Participant Overview Dashboard

The "Participants at our site" section provides a comprehensive view of all participants in your study:

1. **Practice vs. Real Participants**

   - Easily distinguish between training (dummy) accounts and actual study participants
   - Keep track of practice sessions used for staff training
   - Maintain clear separation between test and real participant data

2. **Participation Status Tracking**
   - View active participants currently enrolled in the study
   - Identify participants who have completed or exited the trial
   - Monitor overall study progression and participant retention
