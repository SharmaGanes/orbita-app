## Success Story

The Orbita App has revolutionized how Imperial NHS University Cardiology Group collects patient-reported outcomes. By enabling daily symptom tracking through an intuitive interface, we've achieved:

- **Higher patient compliance** in daily symptom reporting
- **Better quality data** for research outcomes
- **Reduced administrative burden** on research staff
- **Improved patient engagement** throughout clinical trials

## Growing Beyond Imperial

Following our success within Imperial NHS University, we're expanding to support other institutions:

- Proven track record in cardiology research
- Scalable infrastructure ready for multi-center studies
- Customizable framework for different research needs
- Comprehensive training and support documentation
